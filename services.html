In Detail

Facility Highlights

📊

Conference & Banquet Hall

Host your corporate meetings, seminars, weddings, and special events in our elegant banquet hall with a seating capacity of up to 200 guests. Equipped with HD projector, sound system, and dedicated event planning support.

🛜

High-Speed WiFi Connectivity

We provide complimentary high-speed WiFi (100 Mbps) seamlessly across all rooms, corridors, restaurants, and public areas. Ideal for remote workers, video calls, and streaming entertainment.

🗺️

Concierge & Travel Desk

Our experienced concierge team helps you make the most of your time in Jeypore. From booking tickets and arranging cabs to planning day trips to Kolab Dam and tribal villages — we handle it all.

🅿️

Secure Free Parking

We offer ample complimentary parking space for cars and two-wheelers within our secured premises. Our parking facility is monitored 24/7 by CCTV cameras for your vehicle's safety.

🔔

24/7 Room Service

Enjoy a curated in-room dining menu available round-the-clock. Whether it's a late-night snack or a refreshment, our kitchen is always open and ready to serve you at your door.
